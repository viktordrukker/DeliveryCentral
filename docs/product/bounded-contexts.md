# Bounded Contexts

## Identity & Access

Owns authentication, authorization, access policy, and identity accounts.

## Organization & Org Chart

Owns people, org units, reporting lines, and manager scope over time.

## Project Registry

Owns canonical internal projects and their external links.

## Assignment & Workload

Owns formal staffing assignments, approvals, and workload/capacity calculations.

## Time & Work Evidence

Owns observed time and work evidence records separate from assignments.

## Onboarding / Offboarding / Case Management

Owns lifecycle cases and the process coordination around staffing and access impacts.

## Integrations Hub

Owns provider adapters, sync orchestration, and anti-corruption translation.

## Notifications

Owns notification requests, templates, preferences, and delivery records.

## Audit & Observability

Owns immutable audit storage and operational telemetry views.

## Customization / Metadata

Owns custom field definitions, validation schema, and metadata configuration by entity type.
